Goals of the Platform

  1. 01 Simplified Portfolio Management: Centralizes management of stocks, cryptocurrencies, and other assets in one platform.
  2. 02 Integrated Data Sources: Connects to Binance, Interactive Brokers, and manual entries for a complete asset view.
  3. 03 Actionable Insights: Provides real-time metrics, historical trends, and AI-driven forecasts.
  4. 04 Customizable User Experience: Allows creation of personalized wallets and dashboards tailored to strategies.
  5. 05 Secure API Connections: Enables easy integration with brokers, with advanced access controls and authentication.
  6. 06 Collaboration & Sharing: Facilitates portfolio sharing and real-time updates among teams and stakeholders.

To develop an intuitive and efficient CRM, Trade Scope CRM, designed for streamlined portfolio management and analytics in the venture investment market. The platform integrates data, offers actionable insights, and provides a customizable interface for users to optimize their strategies and decision-making.
